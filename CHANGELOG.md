## 1.0.0

- Added `showSpringBottomSheet`, a `showModalBottomSheet`-style route API.
- Added a reusable `SpringBottomSheet` widget with spring physics, snap points,
  drag gestures, rubber-band bounds, and backdrop dismissal.
- Added `SpringBottomSheetController` for imperative snap and dismiss actions.
- Added dependency-free `SpringStaggeredListView` and `SpringStaggeredItem`
  widgets for bottom-up staggered content animations.
- Added an example app demonstrating snap points, dismissal, and staggered
  content.
